Upcoming Events

Permanent Exhibition Made by Hellas

On the occasion of the Greek Presidency of the Council of the European Union, Hellenic Motor Museum is organizing the exhibition, “MADE BY HELLAS” for cars and motorcycles, manufactured, assembled or designed in Greece as well as cars made abroad with the contribution of Greek experts in the field of car industry, entrepreneurs or investors.

Permanent Exhibition Transparency

The permanent exhibition, is a creation of the founder of the Hellenic Motor Museum, Mr. Theodoros Charagionis; it aspires to give the possibility to museum’s visitors to familiarize with the technology of a car and the different kinds of transmission as a determining factor for better driving behavior and consequently reduced car accidents.
